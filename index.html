Instructions

Believe in the heart of the cards!

  • Have the highest total score and win the game!
  • Click 'New Game' to start a new game
  • Click 'Roll the dice' to begin a round

Rules

The game has a total of 3 rounds. Accumulate points in each round.

The player with the highest score at the end of the game wins.

  • Your score is the total sum of your dice pair
  • Rolling any 1 results in a score of 0
  • If you roll a matching pair, the score is the die value multiplied by 4
